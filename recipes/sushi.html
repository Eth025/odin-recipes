Sushi

Description

Ingredients

Some ingredients for this recipe include:
  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Your favourite fillings (such as chicken, tuna, salmon)
  • Sushi Roller

Steps

  1. Place down a bamboo mat and spread a plastic sheet over it.
  2. Place a sheet of seaweed on the plastic wrap.
  3. Add sushi rice to the seaweed.
  4. Add fillings in the center of the sushi rice
  5. Lift the mat and roll, shaping the ingredients into a tube and rolling with pressure.
  6. Roll until an inch of the sushi paper remains, then seal the edge with the rest of the roll.
