Fruit Salad

Description

Ingredients

Some ingredients for this recipe include:
  • Strawberries
  • Pineapple
  • Grapes
  • Blueberries
  • Kiwi
  • Oranges
  • Mangoes
  • Honey Lime Dressing

Steps

  1. Prepare the fruit, cut up into bite sized pieces.
  2. Throw into a bowl.
  3. Toss around to coat the fruit in the dressing, and serve.
