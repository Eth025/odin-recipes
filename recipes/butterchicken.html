Butter Chicken

Description

Ingredients

Some ingredients for this recipe include:
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Diced Chicken Fillets/Breast
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of Tomato Sauce
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garam Masala
  • Oil
  • Tandoori Masala

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 190 degrees F.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet and stir in onion and garlic.
  3. Cook slowly until onion caramelises to a dark brown.
  4. Combine cream, tomato sauce, remaining butter, salt, pepper, garam masala into a sauce pan and bring to simmer.
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Stir onions.
  6. Toss chicken with oil until coated and season with tandoori masala and spread out onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake chicken in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center.
  8. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 5 minutes before serving.
